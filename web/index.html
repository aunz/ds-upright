How it works

Each frame captured from the video stream is first processed using a posenet network to calculate coordiations of 17 key points, such as eyes, nose, ankles. These points are then feed through a convolution neural network which recognises your postures and give them scores.

A score close to 0 means you are upright, a score close to 1 means you are not. So try to get a score as close to 0 as possible.
